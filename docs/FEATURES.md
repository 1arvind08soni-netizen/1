# Features Documentation

## Billing & Sales Management
- Create and manage sales invoices
- Multiple payment modes support
- Payment tracking and receipts
- Sales history and reports

## Inventory Management
- Track gold and silver stock
- Product creation with automatic barcode generation
- Stock updates on purchase and sale
- Batch management
- Real-time inventory tracking

## Client Account Management
- Maintain detailed client profiles
- Track sales and payment history
- Credit/due management
- Client contact information
- Payment tracking

## Purchase Management
- Record gold and silver purchases from suppliers
- Track purchase costs
- Supplier management
- Purchase invoices and receipts
- Automatic stock updates

## Quotation System
- Create quotations for clients (buying/selling)
- Quote expiry tracking
- Convert quotes to orders
- Quote history
- Quote status management

## Money Lending Accounting
- Record client loans
- Track loan details (amount, interest, duration)
- Payment schedules
- Loan status tracking
- Interest calculations
- Monthly payment tracking

## Financial Accounting
- Profit & Loss statements
- Cash flow tracking
- Daily/Monthly/Yearly reports
- Tax calculations
- Revenue analysis
- Expense tracking

## Barcode/Tag System
- Auto-generate unique product tags
- Barcode printing
- Product identification
- Batch barcode generation

## Reports & Analytics
- Sales summary reports
- Inventory reports
- Client ledgers
- Daily financial summaries
- Monthly summaries
- Profit and loss analysis
- Stock movement reports

## User Management
- User authentication
- Role-based access control
- User activity tracking
- Profile management

## Security Features
- JWT-based authentication
- Password encryption
- Role-based authorization
- Activity logging
